# About me:
## I am a postdoctoral researcher in text & data mining at the University of Edinburgh. Currently, I'm working on a project exploring generative AI in the heritage sector. As a digital humanist with an interdisciplinary background, I was originally trained as a literary scholar and completed my PhD at the Unviersity of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. While I'm mainly focused on research in my current post, I have taught undergraduate and postgraduate courses in literature, writing, film, digital humanities, and natural language processing.
